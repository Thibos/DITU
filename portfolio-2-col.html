Business goals & objectives

The aim of DITU Engineering Training is to implement the strategic approach to equipping individuals with skills which will stand them in good stead for future employments. This requirement forms a cornerstone of the corporate responsibility in terms of section 4 of the Social Labour Plan (Local Economic Development).

Growth strategy

  • Identify, Plan, design and determine practical application for identified skills programs, with target population in mind, internally and with external subject matter training providers where required
  • Plan, design and initiate the layout of appropriate skill facilities to suit each skills program
  • Plan, populate, initiate and negotiate approval of Funds with all role players in mind
  • Establish training facilities by means of gathering training resources from local companies and abroad
  • Plan marketing strategy for all interventions, initial internal clients and future external clients (Communicated with stakeholders through Communication networks)
  • Plan, design and initiate Service level agreements with all relevant Stakeholders

Ensure DITU Engineering penetration into client footprint and beyond

  • Effective communications with stakeholders and substantial stakeholder meetings
  • More Frequent visits to existing clientele to determine current shortfalls and expand needs
  • Structured Advertisements in local and national newspapers on programs we deliver
  • Invite more role players/ interested parties/ stakeholders to visit the centre on more frequent bases.
  • Negotiate and obtain SLA`s with stakeholders
  • Be visible at EXPO`s in and around Gauteng
