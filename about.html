Company History

DITU Engineering Training Centre was a concept that started in 2012 when the owners saw the gap of skills shortage in the community. The unemployment rate was/is so high that we saw an opportunity to develop individuals with skills for them to earn a better living and in turn have a better community

What we do

The Centre caters for registered SAQA qualifications in Mechanical, Electrical and will be expanded to also cover mining skills and leadership Skills on a later stage

Require license to operate from SETA’s (MQA, MERSETA , ESETA, EWSETA & TETA) in regards with Accreditation pillars.

  • Training Material
  • ISO Systems
  • Registered Assessors
  • Registered Moderators

Require license to operate from ISO Management Systems.

  • ISO 9001/2008 - Systems
  • ISO 18001 – Safety
  • ISO 14001 - Environmental
